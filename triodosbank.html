Triodos Bank

Language: English & Spanish
Platform: App
Tools: Figma & Notion
Service: Product design

Triodos Bank aims to transform its customers' digital experience by integrating the Bizum service into its app.

Goal

Strengthen the relationship with users by incorporating an instant transfer service (Bizum) within the app.

Challenges

· Educate users on how to use this new service and minimize the learning curve through clear and intuitive content, interaction, and visual design.

· Integrate the functionality in a way that is consistent with the rest of the app's services, respecting its identity and interaction patterns.

· Ensure compliance with user data protection and security standards.

Process

Analysis & Definition
· User needs and pain points analysis.
· Solution definition and functional documentation.
IA & Interaction design
· Information architecture design, interactions, and navigation flows
‍
UI & Prototiping
· Component design and final prototype.

1. Analysis & Definition

Based on insights gathered about home claim assistance, we identified pain points that helped define solutions to add value to the service and increase customer satisfaction.

Contact management

Integration of the contact list and differentiation of contacts who use Bizum.

Display contacts with whom the user interacts the most.

E-commerce

Integration with e-commerce: Payments in online stores using authentication codes.

Payment history and detailed record of all completed purchases.

Notifications and alerts

Notifications to inform about money receptions and requests.

Sending a code to validate operations and enhance security.

Settings & preferences

Allow switching the bank account linked to Bizum.

Option to temporarily enable/disable the service.

2. IA & Interaction design

The design of the AI and interaction system was carried out with two main objectives:
1. Making information easier to find and understand through an intuitive and accessible structure
2. Optimised usability by creating flows aligned with the App’s navigation patterns.

3. UI & Prototyping

I designed the components based on the defined interactions and Triodos Bank’s style guide, ensuring their reusability across different service contexts and delivering a more consistent and seamless user experience.

4. Results

+ 37%

Increase in the number of transfers made through the app.

+ 12%

User retention following the launch of the service.

+ 31%

of users who rescheduled appointments did so through the platform without needing phone assistance.
