Santalucia Seguros

Language: Spanish
Platform: Mobile
Tools: Figma
Service: Product design

Santalucía is an insurance company that offers home insurance among other products. It provides personalized solutions to offer customers protection and peace of mind in the event of home-related incidents.

Goal

Provide customers with continuous and up-to-date information about the status of their home claim, facilitating communication between the company and the customer to strengthen their connection to the process.

Challenges

· Centralize information about each stage of the claim and provide the customer with context on where they are in the process.

· Create a bidirectional communication channel to enhance interaction between the customer and the company.

· Provide easily consumable information and foster a sense of control throughout the claims process by enabling informed decision-making.

Analysis & Definition

Based on the needs identified during client interviews, we defined solutions that would bring utility and value to the product.

Insight
User need
Actionable solution

• "...I need to know what’s going on with my claim but no one tells me anything. I don’t even know if it’s stuck or what..."

• Clear and up-to-date information.

• Provide permanent access to the claim status and next steps directly from the platform.

• “...I have to call several times just to solve a doubt, it’s quite slow…”

• Direct and simplified communication.

• Enable an inmediate channel (WhatsApp) with real-time assistance.

• “...Sometimes I forget the technician’s visit and I’m not at home. If they notified me earlier or if I could reschedule, it would be much easier…”

• Manage appointments easily and without uncertainty.

• Integrate a scheduling system with automatic reminders that allows users to modify or cancel visits without intermediaries.

• “...I feel like I’m out of the process and nobody explains what happens next…”

• Understand the progress and feel part of the process.

• Create a claim timeline that shows the current status and next steps.
